Overview

Purpose

The website represents a Vietnamese restaurant. It provides customers with essential information about the restaurant, including featured dishes, celebration options, menu, and customers'feedback. It also allows customers to place orders for their favorite dishes.

Audience

The website is designed for people of all ages who love Vietnamese food, as well as those who want to place orders for special celebrations.

Dynamic elements

This website includes a slideshow presenting featured dishes of the restaurant.
When the "Place an order" is clicked, a registration form shows up, allowing them to submit an order.
The feedback form asks customers for their feedback and send a thank-you message when they submit the form.
On the Menu page, customers can add their favorite dishes to their cart, and an alert message appears confirming that the dish has been successfully added to the cart.

Content

Home page

1. Welcome to Vietfood
Come and visit us to experience the authentic taste of Vietnamese food. With over 20 years of expertise, we take pride in serving dishes that bring the rich flavors and traditions of Vietnam straight to your table.

2. Our featured dishes
- Pho: Craving something warm on a cold day? A bowl of Pho with rich, flavorful broth, tender beef, and silky noodles will feel like a warm hug—or even a perfect hangover cure!
- Banhmi: Banh Mi—a crispy baguette filled with flavorful meats, fresh veggies, and our secret sauce—is a must-try for those who love convenience but still want a meal packed with flavor.
- Com tam: Looking for something simple yet full of flavor? Cơm Tấm is the perfect meal for you! Enjoy the taste of home-cooked Vietnamese food with broken rice, grilled pork, egg cake, and tangy fish sauce.

3. Celebrations
- Anniversary: Romantic dinner with candlelight settings are perfect for your anniversary. Make your anniversary unforgettable with a cozy, romantic setting and mouthwatering Vietnamese cuisine at Vietfood.
- Family Gatherings: Nothing beats a warm family gathering and delicious food. Come enjoy our traditional Vietnamese home-cooked dishes and share joy and laughter with your loved ones.
- Holidays: The holidays are a time for joy and relaxation—so let us handle the cooking! We're here to serve you during the holidays. Take a break, visit us, and enjoy delicious Vietnamese food.

4. What our customers say
- Anna S. "The Phở was incredible—flavorful and just the right balance of ingredients. The service was fantastic, and the ambiance made it feel like I was in Vietnam. Will definitely be back for more!"
- Jacob H. "We ordered the holiday catering package for New Year’s Eve, and it was outstanding. The food was fresh, flavorful, and beautifully presented. Vietfood made our celebration extra special!"

Menu

1. Appetizer
Egg rolls:crispy rolls stuffed with ground pork, bean thread noodles, wood ear mushrooms and jicama
Chicken salad:shredded chicken and cabbage, along with fresh herbs, carrots, and onion
Summer rolls: fresh rolls packed with rice vermicelli, lettuce, shredded carrots, pork and shrimp

2. Main Course
Pho: beef noodles soup with rich bone broth, rice noodles and thinly sliced beef
Banhmi: Vietnamese sandwich filled with grilled pork, pate, and pickled vegetables
Com tam: broken rice and grilled pork chops served with egg cakes, pickled vegetables and a dipping sauce

3. Dessert
Vietnamese sweet soup: a traditional dessert made from beans, fruits, glutinous rice, and coconut milk
Sticky rice with ice cream: a sweet and savory dessert made by a mound of warm sticky rice with a scoop of ice cream
